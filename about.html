About Us

Real-World Experience Behind Every Recommendation

Real Intelligence Commissioning and Consulting was built on two decades of hands-on building automation and commissioning work — not just theory.

Founder, Real Intelligence Commissioning and Consulting

Michael Do

Michael is a second-generation controls engineer with more than 20 years in building automation, controls, and commissioning — and more than $50 million in projects delivered along the way.

He most recently served as Managing Director of a top 75 MEP design firm with offices worldwide, leading a business generating approximately $22 million in annual revenue. Drawing on that field-tested expertise, he has now founded Real Intelligence Commissioning and Consulting to bring hands-on commissioning and controls know-how directly to owners, developers, and facility teams — systems engineered right, verified thoroughly, and explained in plain language.
