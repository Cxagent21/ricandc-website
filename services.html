Services

Full-Lifecycle Building Performance Services

From design review through occupancy and beyond, we make sure your building's systems work the way they were meant to.

01

Building Commissioning (Cx)

We verify that HVAC, controls, and building systems are designed, installed, and calibrated to meet the owner's project requirements — for new construction and existing buildings alike. Retro-commissioning engagements identify performance drift in aging systems and restore them to peak efficiency.

02

BAS & Controls Engineering

Design, programming, and troubleshooting of building automation and HVAC control systems, including sequence-of-operations development, submittals, and large-scale network and graphics design for operator interfaces. Programmer-level fluency across 15+ platforms, including Siemens, Johnson Controls, Delta, and Andover, as well as open protocols including BACnet, Modbus, and LON.

03

LEED & Energy Consulting

Testing, documentation, and consulting support for projects pursuing LEED certification or aggressive energy performance targets, ensuring building systems support the sustainability goals set at design.

04

Field Analysis & Systems Optimization

On-site evaluation of existing building sites to identify upgrade opportunities, correct control deficiencies, and improve occupant comfort and energy performance.

05

Project Management

Coordinated management of design, installation, and maintenance for building automation, HVAC, fire, and security systems — including scheduling, forecasting, billing, and multi-contractor coordination on projects ranging from single buildings to multi-million dollar portfolios.

06

Owner's Representation & Advisory

Independent, technically grounded advisory support for owners and developers — helping you evaluate proposals, hold contractors accountable, and make informed decisions from design through occupancy.
